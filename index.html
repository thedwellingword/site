Welcome

The Dwelling Word is that scripture that abides so richly within us, so deeply and continually, that it captivates our minds and our hearts in every aspect of life. It is that word within us that informs every thought toward Christ, stirs up every affection toward Christ, and drives our will such that every word and every action seeks to glorify Christ.

TheDwellingWord.org is a collection of projects and initiatives to inspire, encourage, motivate, and teach God's people to know God deeply through knowing God's word richly. We hope you are blessed by the resources that you find here.
